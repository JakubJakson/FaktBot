FaktBot is a cutting-edge app that helps you determine the credibility of any news article or source. With an increasing amount of information available, it can be difficult to determine what's true and what's not. That's where FaktBot comes in. With just a few taps, our sophisticated algorithm will analyze the article and give you a score based on its credibility. Our mission is to promote media literacy and combat the spread of misinformation. We believe that access to accurate information is a fundamental right and it is our responsibility to provide it to the public.
